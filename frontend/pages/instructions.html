Important Instructions

  • Timer will start as soon as you click Start Typing. Test will auto-submit when time is up.
  • Copy-paste is not allowed. Right-click is disabled during the test.
  • Type the passage exactly as shown. Extra spaces and punctuation errors are counted as mistakes.
  • Use only one space after punctuation marks (comma, period, etc.).
  • Do not switch tabs or minimize the browser during the test. Tab switch is detected.
  • For Hindi typing, ensure Mangal font is installed and Inscript keyboard is enabled.
  • Do not refresh the page during the test. Your progress will be lost.
  • WPM and accuracy are calculated in real-time. Results are saved automatically after submission.
