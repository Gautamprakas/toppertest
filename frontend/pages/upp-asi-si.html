UPP SI & ASI Selection Process — Written Exam + Typing Test

Prepare for both stages of Uttar Pradesh Police Sub Inspector & Assistant Sub Inspector recruitment in one place

Selection for the Uttar Pradesh Police SI (Sub Inspector) and UPP ASI (Assistant Sub Inspector) posts is a multi-stage process conducted by the Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB). Aspirants first clear a written examination covering General Hindi, General Knowledge, Numerical & Mental Ability and Mental Aptitude / IQ / Reasoning, and — for ministerial posts such as ASI (Clerk) and ASI (Accounts) — a computer typing test in Hindi (Mangal font, Inscript layout) and English. Many hard-working candidates clear the written exam but lose their rank at the typing stage simply because they never practiced in the real exam format.

TopperTest covers both stages. Practice the UPP SI typing test and UPP ASI typing test with real exam-pattern passages, the correct Inscript keyboard layout and instant WPM & accuracy reports — then attempt full-length UP Police ASI mock tests and SI written-exam practice sets built on the latest exam pattern. Every passage and mock is prepared by ASI Sandeep, a serving officer and YouTube educator who has guided lakhs of Uttar Pradesh Police aspirants through typing and written-exam preparation.
