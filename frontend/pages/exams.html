Frequently Asked Questions

How is WPM calculated in TopperTest?
WPM (Words Per Minute) is calculated as total words typed divided by time taken in minutes. For example, if you type 250 words in 10 minutes, your WPM = 250/10 = 25 WPM.
Which font is used for Hindi typing test?
TopperTest uses Mangal Unicode font with Inscript keyboard layout for Hindi typing tests, which is the standard used in all government exams like UP Police, High Court, and DSSSB.
Is TopperTest free to use?

How do I improve my typing speed for SSC?
Practice daily for at least 1-2 hours. Focus on accuracy first, then speed. Use TopperTest's real exam passages to get familiar with the type of content in actual exams. Watch ASI Sandeep's YouTube videos for tips and keyboard tricks.
