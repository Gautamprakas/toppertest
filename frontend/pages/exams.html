Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typing test pattern for UPP SI, UPP ASI and Computer Operator?
The Uttar Pradesh Police (UPP) SI, ASI and Computer Operator typing test is conducted in Hindi (Mangal font, Inscript keyboard) and/or English depending on the post. TopperTest provides real exam-pattern passages for UPP SI, UPP ASI and Computer Operator so you can practice with the exact format used in the actual Uttar Pradesh Police recruitment exam.
How is WPM calculated in TopperTest?
WPM (Words Per Minute) is calculated as total words typed divided by time taken in minutes. For example, if you type 250 words in 10 minutes, your WPM = 250/10 = 25 WPM.
Which font is used for Hindi typing test?
TopperTest uses Mangal Unicode font with Inscript keyboard layout for Hindi typing tests, which is the standard used in all government exams like UP Police, High Court, and DSSSB.
