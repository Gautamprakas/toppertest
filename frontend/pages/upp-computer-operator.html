UP Police Computer Operator — Written Exam + Typing Test

Prepare for both stages of UPP Computer Operator recruitment in one place

Recruitment for the UP Police Computer Operator post, conducted by the Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB), tests candidates in two key stages: a written examination focused on computer knowledge — computer fundamentals, hardware and software, MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint), internet and networking basics — along with general aptitude, and a computer typing test in Hindi (Mangal font, Inscript layout) and English. The typing stage is where well-prepared candidates often slip, because typing under exam pressure is a skill that only comes from format-accurate practice.

TopperTest prepares you for both. Practice the UP Police Computer Operator typing test with real exam-pattern passages, the correct Inscript keyboard layout and instant WPM & accuracy reports — then attempt full-length a fresh daily challenge passage every day to keep your typing practice consistent and exam-ready.
